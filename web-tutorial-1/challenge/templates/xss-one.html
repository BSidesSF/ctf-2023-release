Can you steal the flag?

You'll need an XSS payload that will bypass the following CSP, 
              
                default-src 'self' 'unsafe-inline'; 
                script-src 'self' 'unsafe-inline';
                connect-src *; 
                style-src-elem 'self' fonts.googleapis.com fonts.gstatic.com; 
                font-src 'self' fonts.gstatic.com fonts.googleapis.com
Notice that unsafe-inline is allowed, try inserting <script>alert(1);</script>
Try to read /xss-one-flag as admin, all payloads submitted here will be sent to the admin.
